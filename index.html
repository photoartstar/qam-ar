Queer American Memorials · New York City
Amanda Milan
November 20, 1974 — June 20, 2000
Amanda Milan was a Black trans woman and beloved member of New York's ballroom community. Murdered outside the Port Authority Bus Terminal at 42nd & 8th Ave, age 25. Her death galvanized the trans rights movement and prompted organizing across the city.
"Death will not be the last word for Amanda Milan."
— Community memorial, Metropolitan Community Church, July 2000
