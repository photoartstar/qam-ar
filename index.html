Queer American Memorials · New York City
Amanda Milan
November 20, 1974 — June 20, 2000
Amanda Milan was a Black trans woman and beloved member of New York's ballroom community. She was murdered outside the Port Authority Bus Terminal on 42nd Street, at the age of 25. Her death galvanized the trans rights movement and prompted renewed organizing across the city.
"Death will not be the last word for Amanda Milan."
— Community memorial, Metropolitan Community Church, July 2000
